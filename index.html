I Am
Tim Berners-Lee

I am the founder of www: World Wide Web. I am a computer scientist, inventor, and creator of one of the most used things in today's world.

Hi!

Tim Berners Lee was born on June 8th, 1955 in England to Conway Berners-Lee and Mary Lee Woods. Tim Berners Lee is most well known for his invention of the famous WWW (World Wide Web). He was at the University of Oxford and is a professional in computer science. He has many different important roles, such as having the 3Com founder's chair at MIT CSAIL (being a senior researcher there as well), being director of the WSRI, and being a member of the advisory board at MIT Center for Collective Intelligence. Some facts about Tim Berners-Lee is that he technically made the first ever website. Adding onto that, he made it out of rage. He was constantly frustrated about logging in and out of different technologies to find information. Tim Berners Lee has changed our world drastically with his invention of the World Wide Web.
